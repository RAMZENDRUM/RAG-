
GODREJ DISHA SKILL DEVELOPMENT CENTRE

# ABOUT

The Godrej Disha Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Skill Development Centre is a state-of-the-art facility dedicated to enhancing the skills and knowledge of individuals in the field of refrigeration and air conditioning. This centre, established by the renowned Godrej Group, serves as a beacon for technical education and professional development. Equipped with cutting-edge training tools and experienced instructors, it offers comprehensive programs aimed at equipping students and technicians with the expertise required in the HVAC-R industry. Whether you're an aspiring technician seeking to kick start your career or a professional looking to upgrade your skills, the Godrej Disha Centre provides the ideal platform. With a strong focus on hands-on training and theoretical knowledge, it ensures that individuals are well-prepared to handle the complexities of modern refrigeration and air conditioning systems. This centre commitment to skill development aligns with industry demands, making it a pivotal institution in the realm of HVAC-R education.

# OBJECTIVE

- Empower individuals with specialized HVAC-R skills
- Bridge the industry skill gap through quality training
- Foster employment opportunities in refrigeration and air conditioning
- Promote sustainable and energy-efficient cooling solutions
- Elevate the standards of HVAC-R expertise in the workforce

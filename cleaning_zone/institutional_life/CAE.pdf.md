# ABOUT

A "Centre for Computer-Aided Engineering” Technology centre is supported by ALTAIR University Global Academic program and AUTODESK. It is a specialized facility dedicated to advancing the field of computer-aided engineering (CAE). This centre serves as hubs for research, development, and innovation in CAE technology. They bring together experts, engineers, and researchers to collaborate on cutting-edge projects, improve simulation and modelling techniques, and develop software tools for engineering analysis and design. This centre plays a vital role in enhancing the efficiency and accuracy of engineering processes across industries such as automotive, aerospace, civil engineering, and more. They serve as a catalyst for the advancement of CAE, driving innovation and knowledge dissemination in the field.

# OBJECTIVE

- Understand and interpret drawings of machine components
- Prepare assembly drawings using standard CAD packages
- Gain practical experience in handling 2D drafting and 3D modeling software systems
- Comprehend, design, analyze, and suggest novel materials, products and solutions for the contemporary manufacturing issues.

The Poster Presentation on Business Plans was conducted on 30th August of 2023 by the Aspiring Entrepreneurs as well as students of Mohamed Sathak AJ College of Engineering.

The Poster Presentation on Business Plans event was a dynamic platform that brought together aspiring entrepreneurs, business students, and industry professionals to showcase their innovative business ideas and strategies. The event aimed to foster creativity, collaboration, and knowledge sharing within the realm of business planning and entrepreneurship.

The main objective of this event was to explore and present innovative and feasible startup ideas that have the potential to disrupt various industries and address real-world problems.

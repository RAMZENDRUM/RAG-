# Write-up on Event

The Department of Information Technology &#x26; IIC of MSAJCE, Chennai jointly organized one day Webinar on the topic “Acceleration – Early Stage Entrepreneurs” on 02/08/2023. The program commenced at 11 AM – 12.30 PM, with welcome address and introduction speech given by Mr. Mohideen Abdul Kader, AHOD-IT Dept of MSAJCE. The Chief guest Mr. A. Aktheer Ilyas, Founder, Creative Director, delivered his speech with a presentation and gave many real-time examples and well explained about the concept of UX Design. Students gained a lot of insights about UX designing. In addition, the chief guest gave tips about developing and managing a startup. The faculties and the students from the department of CSE and IT participated. During the session, totally 150 members actively participated and gained a lot of ideas about UX designing and Startup initiation from the event. At the end of the session, Mr. Mohideen Abdul Kader, AHOD/IT-MSAJCE delivered the vote of thanks.

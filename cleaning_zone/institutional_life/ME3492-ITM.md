Valve case study 

A variation of a pressure -compensated flow control valve is 

a temperature -compensated flow control valve. This 

variation comes because sometimes the temperature of the 

operation may rise such that set tolerances in orifices will 

become inaccurate. Temperature compensators are installed 

to cater to these variations.

# Event Title

# Venue/Mode

# Coordinator

The Department of Civil Engineering organized a program on “Innovation &#x26; Entrepreneurship Outreach Program in Schools / Community” on 17th August, 2023 at 2.30 PM in Government Higher Secondary School at Kelambakkam, with the presence of Program Coordinators Mrs. V. Janaki, AP/Civil &#x26; Mrs. P. Kavithra, AP/Civil. The main objective of the program is to create awareness among students about the Entrepreneurship and Innovative ideas.

In this program, around 90 higher secondary students participated and gained knowledge about Innovative ideas. Mrs. V. Janaki, AP/Civil, motivated the students to build their skills on Entrepreneurship and encouraged the students to create new innovative ideas. Mrs. P. Kavithra, AP/Civil, discussed the significance of innovation and entrepreneurship in day to day life.

The session introduced the students to the world of entrepreneurship and innovation and inspired them to become future leaders in their fields. Mrs. V. Janaki, AP/Civil, and Mrs. P. Kavithra, AP/Civil, delivered the vote of thanks to participants and teachers who contributed to making the event a success. The program ended up with great success.

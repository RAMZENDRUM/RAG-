# Report on Voters Day Awareness program

Date: 27.01.2023

Our NSS unit conducted National Voters Day on 25th January 2023. Teaching and non-teaching faculty members and students participated. Since 2011, National Voters’ Day has been celebrated on January 25 every year, all across the country to mark the foundation day of the Election Commission of India, i.e. 25th January 1950.

It has been started from 25 January 2011 to mark the foundation day of Election Commission of India. The theme for this year’s NVD, ‘Nothing Like Voting, I Vote for Sure’ is dedicated to voters which conveys individual’s feeling and aspiration towards participation in the electoral process through power of their vote. The main purpose of the celebration is to create electoral awareness amongst citizens and encourage them to participate in the electoral process. It is also used to facilitate enrolment of voters, especially the newly eligible young voters.

The main purpose of the NVD celebration is to encourage, facilitate and maximize enrolment, especially for the new voters. Dedicated to the voters of the country, the day is utilized to spread awareness among voters and for promoting informed participation in the electoral process.

Dr. A. Balakrishnan, NSS Program Officer addressed the students about the relevance and importance of celebrating this day and encouraged them to participate in the electoral process. All college staff members and students had taken Voters Pledge, to uphold the democratic traditions of our country and the dignity of free, fair and peaceful elections. Dr. Rajakumar, HOD/S&#x26;H coordinated the program and made all the NSS volunteers, students and teachers to take the oath in room no. 306.

Description 

Design of belt drive concept is explained by role -play exercises by 

the students. The students assumed the role of the following, 

Student 1: Types of belt drive. 

Student 2: Material Selection. 

Student 3: Application of belt drive. 

The students performed their roles in front of their classmates. This 

activity helped the students understand the process of planning 

activities.

# Write-up on Event:

The seminar on “Innovation and Startup Ecosystem Enablers from the region level” conducted by the Department of Training and Placement and Entrepreneur Development Cell associated with IIC on August 24, 2023, successfully delivered comprehensive training about enabling startup from regional level. The seminar had an impressive attendance of around 30 students. The resource person, Mr. Suriya Prakash from the startup company Mechimed, delivered a speech on innovation and startup ecosystem enablers from the region level. The Seminar provided valuable insights into the practical aspects of innovations in the startup and process of enabling startup.

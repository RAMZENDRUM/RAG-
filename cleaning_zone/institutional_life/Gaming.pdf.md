
CENTRE FOR GAMING AND AR/VR

# ABOUT:

A "Gaming and AR &#x26; VR" Technology Centre is a specialized hub or institution dedicated to fostering innovation, research, and development in the fields of gaming, augmented reality (AR), and virtual reality (VR). These centres bring together experts, developers, and industry professionals to collaborate on cutting-edge projects, share knowledge, and advance the state-of-the-art in gaming and immersive technologies. They serve as a catalyst for driving industry growth, pushing the boundaries of interactive entertainment, and exploring new possibilities in AR and VR applications across various sectors, from gaming to education, healthcare, and beyond.

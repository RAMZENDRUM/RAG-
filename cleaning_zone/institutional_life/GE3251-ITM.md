Topic  Prism & Pyramid – Model Making 

Description 

A pyramid is a three -dimensional polyhedron -shaped 

structure with only one polygonal base and has triangular 

sides. A prism is a three -dimensional polyhedron 

characterized by two bases that are polygonal in shape and 

rectangular sides perpendicular to the base. Model Making 

can promote deeper engagement with the content of 

Engineer ing Graphics Basics of development of surfaces .

# Report on Blood donation camp

Date: 20.05.2023

A Blood donation camp was organized by our NSS unit, in collaboration with Tamilnadu Dr.M.G.R. Medical University, through which more than 120 units of blood was donated by both students and the staff members of our college from 9 am to 2 pm on 17.05.2023.

Our College has been religiously organizing blood donation camps every year and students and the faculty members come forward voluntarily to donate blood. A group of 15 people from the Dr.M.G.R.University successfully did all the process in a very safe and secured manner. While addressing to the students, the camp officers and other medical attendants informed clearly about the process of the donation and advised the students regarding the importance of blood donation. Total number of donors are 61 and the total number of units collected are 34 in which the total number of deferrals are 27.

Blood donation can save lives of innumerable persons. Donation of blood is very critical and crucial for saving lives many patients and those who have met with accidents. It is a way of our college gesture in bringing a ray of hope to contribute to the serious problems of acutes shortage of blood. India faces a blood shortage of 3 million units.

In the camp our NSS volunteers brought awareness in them about shortage of blood and why we must donate blood every year, to help the persons requiring blood. It is as such a great service or contribution to the society and people living in it. The Blood donation camp was successfully completed with the coordination of the NSS coordinators.

# Report on Tobacco Awareness Rally

Date : 30.06.2023

Our college NSS unit organized a Tobacco Awareness Rally on 26.06.2023. The rally created awareness among the general public on the harmful effects of tobacco and general health.

The coordinators flagged off an awareness rally at the entrance of Sipcot and also in Navalur. The rally passed through the major streets of the city. Nearly 50 NSS volunteers enthusiastically participated and raised their voices against the use of tobacco. They chanted awareness slogans such as ‘Give up tobacco, choose life’, and ‘Smoking cigarettes means death’.

These sessions sensitize students to the dangers of tobacco use and its detrimental impact on health and society. In support of the cause, students participated enthusiastically in the rally, showcasing posters and distributing handouts to spread the message.

The rally was headed by Dr. Suresh, AP/EEE and Mr. Stephen Raj, AP/Physics coordinated the students for distributing the pamphlets and chanting the slogans.

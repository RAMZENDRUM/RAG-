# Write-up on Workshop Event

Angel Investors play a pivotal role in the startup eco-system, providing financial support and mentorship to early stage ventures. The main objective of this seminar was to discuss – Generating Ideas, capturing ideas, beginning innovation, developing a business-effectiveness strategy, Applying Business Improvement.

The session focused on the innovation development and understanding technology readiness level, manufacturing readiness level and investment readiness level. The speaker first explained about innovation development and investments and the various processes involved in developing a new product. Also explained the various stages in detail and emphasized on those stages where the mentors can provide the required guidance to students.

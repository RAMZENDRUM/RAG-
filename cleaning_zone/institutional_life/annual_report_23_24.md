positions in various esteemed organizations. 

We engage professional trainers to prepare our final year students for placement,

The college nurtures healthy relationship with its alumni by inducting all the final 

year students into it. We have formally registered alumni association under

Alumni have actively supported the institution by organizing webinars, engaging

# Report on Dental Health Awareness Camp

Date: 12.01.2023

Our NSS unit with the team of Dr. R. Riaz, MDS from Tagore Dental College and Hospital conducted a Dental Health awareness Camp on 11.01.2023 for faculty members and students.

Tagore Dental College and Hospital is helping the society by giving Dental Health awareness camp in rural areas, Educational Institutions and villages every year. The camp began with a demo session about the dental health and hygiene to be maintained by the people.

The doctor demonstrated about the dental issues and the remedies to be taken by the people. He also educated all the students on the necessity for dental care and regular check-up every six months.

The faculty members and the students were also asked to take up the dental check-up. Nearly 80 students participated and got benefitted by the camp. The objective of this event was to spread awareness on teeth disorder and ailments for the working people. The doctors and his team took care of everyone in an outstanding way and efficiently conducted the dental health camp for the students and staff members.

Dental examination and basic treatment awareness was also given to the students who need immediate treatment. Recommendations for further treatment and maintenance of the teeth and oral health and follow-up were given. It was very helpful for the needy people who are struggling with long-time dental problems. The NSS volunteers played a vital role in making this camp successful with their service.

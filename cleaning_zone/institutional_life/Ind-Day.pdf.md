# Write-up on Event

Our college observed the 77ᵗʰ Independence Day with dignity and honour. The ceremony was started at 8AM in our college premises with faculty and students. In the presence of a gleeful gathering, the National Flag was hoisted by Dr.G.Ramesh Head of Exam Cell. As soon as the flag was hoisted, everyone sang the National Anthem. At the end, the packets of sweets were distributed to the students and faculty.

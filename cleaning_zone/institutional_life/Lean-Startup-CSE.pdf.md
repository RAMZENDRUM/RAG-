The Department of Computer Science and Engineering &#x26; IIC of MSAJCE, Chennai jointly organized a one-day Seminar on the topic “Lean Startup and Minimum Viable Product/Business” on 23/08/2023. The program commenced at 10 AM, with a welcome address and introduction speech given by Dr. Vijayarangan, HoD-CSE Dept of MSAJCE. The Chief guest, Prof. Dr. Gopal Sivakumar, CEO, Vision Excellence delivered his speech with a presentation and provided many real-time examples, well explaining the concept of Lean Six Sigma. Students gained a lot of insights about Lean Six Sigma. In addition, the chief guest provided tips about developing and managing a startup. The faculties and the students from the department of CSE and IT participated. During the session, a total of 100 members actively participated and gained many ideas about Lean Six Sigma and Startup initiation from the event. At the end of the session, Mr. V. Pandarinathan, AHOD/CSE- MSAJCE delivered the vote of thanks.

Topic Process Model design 

Description 

A software process model, also known as a software 

development life cycle (SDLC) model, is a structured 

approach used in software engineering to guide the 

development of software applications or systems. These 

models provide a systematic and organized framework for 

managing the various stages of software development, from 

initial planning and requirements gathering to testing, 

deployment, and maintenance.

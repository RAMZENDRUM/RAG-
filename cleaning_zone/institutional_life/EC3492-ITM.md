Description 

It results in the  develop ment of  problem -solving and presentation 

skills, excellent team spirit, increase research skills, self -

confidence, attitudinal change, and sense of achievement in the 

students. 

Students were given a set of problems related to  Real time 

applications of signal processing . They were asked to write a 

program and  execute  it in MATLAB.  They were instructed to 

present the output for discussion and submit a report.

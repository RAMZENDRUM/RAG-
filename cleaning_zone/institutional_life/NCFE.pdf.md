# Report on Financial Education Program of NCFE

Date: 20.03.2023

Our college NSS unit organized a Financial Education Program of NCFE on 16.03.2023. Mr. S.N.Jayabalan, trainer, gave a presentation and delivered a speech on the financial planning for the non-teaching staff members. Personal Financial Planning is a significant issue; however, not many people are aware of the appropriate ways to address it. The program was therefore aimed at giving insights to the participants about the vital aspects of financial planning.

The targeted participants of the program were non-teaching faculty members, lab instructors, office bearers, and other maintenance in charges. A total number of 40 members participated in the program and benefited from the information shared by the speaker.

The speaker began his session briefing about the importance of Investment in Precious Metals and Mutual Funds by discussing the benefits of investing in precious metals like gold and silver and the modes of investing in them. Thereafter, he gave a brief account of the concept of mutual funds, types of mutual funds. He also discussed the return potential of mutual funds, tax benefits, and modes of investments in mutual funds.

The speaker emphasized the necessity of health insurance in the wake of rising incidence of diseases and cost of healthcare and also explained the important aspects of Medi-claim Policies and issues to be considered while opting for different riders in the policy. During the session, participants were invited to express their thoughts about learning derived from the session and also share their views on different aspects of personal finance.

The program was organized by the NSS coordinator Dr. A.Balakrishnan, Head, Academics. He thanked the chief guest for the helpful assistance and the non-teaching staff members for their enthusiastic cooperation.

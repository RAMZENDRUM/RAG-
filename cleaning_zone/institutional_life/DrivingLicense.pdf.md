# Report on Driving License Awareness camp

Date: 14.12.2022

Parveen Travels, Chennai conducted a Driving License Awareness camp with the initiative of our college NSS unit on 12.12.2022 and 13.12.2022 for faculty members and students. During the camp the resource persons from Parveen Travels acquainted the students about road rules and regulations and briefed about the latest information in the field of registration and driving license. The road safety measures and users behaviour were also thoroughly discussed.

The Travels personnel cleared the doubts of the students regarding the registration of driving license and the grievance around the license issues in renewal, name change and new registration. The importance of possessing the driving license and the need to follow the government norms related to driving was also explained by the members. Many students and faculty members attended the camp and got benefitted by the camp.

The NSS volunteers played a vital role in making this camp successful by insisting all the students to visit the camp and get their doubts cleared. Dr. A. Balakrishnan coordinated the camp and thanked the Parveen Travels personnel for their immediate and helpful support on a socially important theme.

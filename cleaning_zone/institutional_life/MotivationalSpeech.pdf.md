# REPORT ON THE MOTIVATIONAL SPEECH

DATE: 10.06.2023

A motivational speech was organised by the Department of Science and Humanities and the NSS cell of our college on 9ᵗʰ June 2023 from 02.00 pm to 3.15 pm in the Alhaj Dhasthagir Auditorium, Ground floor Seminar Hall. The resource person is Ms. Shamini, CEO of Mind Boom, a motivational center and Life Skills Trainer.

This motivational seminar was conducted for all the first year students for personality development. The ultimate goal of a motivational seminar was to change people profoundly on an emotional and mental level. The speaker related her own personal experience to an audience regarding the necessity to be smart, shrewd and maintain good interpersonal relationships to achieve the greatest heights. She advised the students to be refined, updated and respectful in the current scenario.

She interacted with the students to make them speak with boldness and share their perspective towards life. Being a Skill development Trainer, she made the students to speak freely about their dreams and plans in their future without having fear. Her motivation insisted the students to know the power of creativity and presence of mind so that they answered on the spot questions with their imagination.

The program was coordinated by the NSS coordinators and ended successfully with the National anthem.

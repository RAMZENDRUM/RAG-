# ABOUT

The "Artificial Intelligence and Machine learning" training program, offered under the AICTE-KARMA scheme, represents a significant step towards nurturing future-ready talent in cutting-edge technologies. This initiative, supported by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), is designed to equip students and professionals with essential skills in AI and machine learning. Through comprehensive coursework, hands-on projects, and expert guidance, participants gain proficiency in AI algorithms, data analysis, and machine learning techniques. This training not only empowers individuals to excel in today's technology-driven job market but also contributes to the nation's growth by preparing a workforce capable of harnessing the potential of AI and ML across various sectors.

# OBJECTIVE

- Its objective is to equip individuals with AI and ML skills for career enhancement
- The program intends to bridge the skill gap in these emerging technologies.
- It aims to empower students and professionals with practical AI and ML knowledge
- Ultimately, it seeks to contribute to India's technological advancement and workforce development.

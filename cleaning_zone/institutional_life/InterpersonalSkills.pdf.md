# Write-up on Event:

Department of Training and Placement organized one day Seminar on “Intro to Intermed Interpersonal Skills” on 03/12/2022. The program was started at 11:00 AM with a welcome address, honour and introduction to the Chief guest by Ms. Saipriya, Ass Prof / SH - MSAJCE. The Chief guest Mrs. Akila Rajesh, Voora Property Developers Pvt Ltd, delivered her speech with a presentation on how to develop the interpersonal skills. In this session, she discussed about the various skills like adaptability and many other skills to develop their skills. During the discussion time with the participants, she answered and cleared the doubts of participants and participants came forward and gave feedback about the session. At the end of the session, Mr. Vigneshwaran V, AP/Mech-MSAJCE, delivered the vote of thanks.

The Personality Development and Business Etiquette Program for Ladies was conducted to empower women with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in their professional endeavours. To commemorate the World Entrepreneurs Day and Business Women's Day, this program was conducted on 31st of August 2023, which aims to enhance self-confidence, effective communication, foster the spirit to dream big, and take risks to be an entrepreneur and refine social graces, enabling participants to navigate the corporate world with poise and professionalism. The Program Convenor Dr. D. Weslin spoke about the need to enhance confidence and effectiveness in the workplace in their felicitation address.

"Entrepreneur is someone who has a vision for something and a want to create." With this positive quote, the chief guest Mrs. Renuka, Thought Leader, spoke about the need to have a great vision to be successful in our life. She spoke about how the entrepreneurs drive.

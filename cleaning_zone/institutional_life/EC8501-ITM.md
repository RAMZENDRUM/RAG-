d) Both b) and c) are correct

a) The waveform has zero value for symbol '0' b) The waveform has A volts for symbol '1' 

c) The waveform has positive and negative values for '1' and '0' symbol respectively 

d) Both (a) and (b) are correct

a) 1 is transmitted by a positive pulse and 0 is transmitted by negative pulse b) 1 is

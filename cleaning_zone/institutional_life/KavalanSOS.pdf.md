# Report on Kavalan SOS App Awareness Program

Date: 24.04.2023

Our college NSS unit organized an awareness programme in the college auditorium to explain about the benefits of the KAVALAN SOS App on 20.04.2023. It is an app launched by the Tamilnadu Police as a part of its Master Control Room initiative and this service is available throughout the day for the benefit of the youngsters or girls who are in trouble. It enables the users to alert the police immediately during emergency situations such as assault, kidnap, eve teasing and natural disaster.

The working system of the app was demonstrated by the police personnel elaborately. They helped the students to download the app first and explained about the function keys of the Kavalan SOS app. The police advised the students to make use of the app at the time of problems and safety issues. And gave hope that the police officials will immediately respond to the people and take necessary action for the safety of the students. The police personnel interacted with the students and faculty members and cleared their doubt regarding the response of the police and the usage of the SOS app.

The program was organised by Dr. A. Balakrishnan, NSS program officer. The Coordinators Mr. Simon Jeyasundar, AP/Physics and Mr. Stephen Raj, AP/Physics coordinated the students and helped everyone to download the app and assured to the police about the download of all the students.

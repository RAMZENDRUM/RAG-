# Women’s Day Celebration Week

Women’s day celebration week started on March 1st 2023 with an awareness program for our girl students and women faculties along with girl students and women faculties from Mohamed Sathak Nursing College. Hence awareness about Silambam has been delivered through our chief guest Mr. R.K. Sarath, Silambam National Referee along with Mrs. Kalpana Suresh, Special Educator and Counsellor. They gave awareness about Silambam and self-confidence for girls. Welcome address and chief guest address were delivered by our students and then chief guests were honoured with gifts and shawls. Then Mr. Kishore, III year Mech, MSAJCE gave brief explanation about Silambam and delivered a demo on Silambam along with a video. Then the program came to an end by delivering vote of thanks.

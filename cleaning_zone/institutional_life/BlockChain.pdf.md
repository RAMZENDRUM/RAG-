
# CENTRE FOR BLOCK CHAIN TECHNOLOGY

(Supported By Bharat Blockchain Network)

# ABOUT

The collaboration between Bharat Blockchain Network and Mohamed Sathak A.J. College of Engineering marks a significant milestone in the realm of blockchain education. With a shared vision of advancing blockchain technology and its applications, this partnership aims to offer a comprehensive range of blockchain-related courses to empower students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in this rapidly evolving field. The primary objective of this collaboration is to bridge the gap between academia and industry by providing students with practical insights into blockchain technology. Through a curated curriculum designed by experts from both organizations, students will gain hands-on experience in blockchain development, smart contract creation, decentralized applications, and the security aspects of blockchain systems. This collaboration seeks to foster innovation by encouraging research and development in blockchain technology. Students will have access to cutting-edge resources and mentorship opportunities, enabling them to explore blockchain's potential in various industries, such as finance, supply chain, healthcare, and more. Ultimately, this partnership aspires to produce a new generation of blockchain experts who can drive innovation, solve complex problems, and contribute to the growth of the blockchain ecosystem in India and beyond. It embodies the spirit of collaboration, education, and innovation that is essential in the ever-evolving world of blockchain technology.

# OBJECTIVE

- The Centre for Blockchain Technology aims to be a pioneering hub for blockchain research and development.
- Our objective is to promote blockchain adoption across industries by fostering innovation and education.
- We strive to create a collaborative ecosystem, bringing together experts, students, and industry leaders.
- The centre goal is to advance the understanding of blockchain's potential and its real-world applications.
- Through research, training, and partnerships, we aim to contribute to the growth and maturation of the global blockchain ecosystem.

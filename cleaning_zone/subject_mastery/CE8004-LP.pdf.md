# COURSE OBJECTIVES

The student should be made to:

The objective of this course is to enable students to have the knowledge on planning process and to introduce to the students about the regulations and laws related to Urban Planning.

# COURSE OUTCOMES

Upon completion of the course, the student should be able to:

- Describe basic issues in urban planning
- Formulate plans for urban and rural development
- Plan and analyse socio economic aspects of urban and rural planning
- Design of urban development projects.
- Manage urban development projects.

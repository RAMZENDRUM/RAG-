# Course Objective

- Introduce the rudiments of plane surveying and geodetic principles to Civil Engineers.
- Learn the various methods of plane and geodetic surveying to solve the real world Civil Engineering problems.
- Introduce the concepts of Control Surveying.
- Introduce the basics of Astronomical Surveying.

# Course Outcome

- Explain uses of various surveying instruments and mapping.
- Solve Horizontal angle and vertical angle using different instruments.
- Demonstrate Methods of Leveling and setting Levels with different instruments.
- Describe astronomical surveying and methods to determine time, longitude, latitude and azimuth.
- Explain the Concept and principle of modern surveying.

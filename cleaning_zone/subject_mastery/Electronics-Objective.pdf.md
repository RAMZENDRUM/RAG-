
# DEPARTMENT OF ELECTRICAL AND ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING

# Name of the Lab: Electronics Laboratory

# OBJECTIVES

- To expose the students to understand the behaviour of semiconductor device based on experimentation.
- To simulate various electric circuits using Pspice / Matlab / e-Sim / Scilab.

# OUTCOMES

At the end of the course, the student should have the:

- Ability to understand and apply circuit theorems and concepts in engineering applications.
- Ability to simulate electric circuits.
- Ability to understand and analyse electronic circuits.

# Course Objective 

To impart knowledge on common method of sub soil investigation and design of foundation. At the end of this course student acquires the capacity to investigate the 

soil condition and to select and design a suitable foundation. 

# Course Outcome 

1. Describe various methods involved in sub soil exploration, Sampler and Sampling 

Techniques 

2.Design the foundation based on the bearing capacity of soil 

3. Analyze to Carry out various test involved in settlement of foundation 

4. Apply the suitable type of footing for different loading conditions. 

5. Analyze various pressure acting on retaining walls
